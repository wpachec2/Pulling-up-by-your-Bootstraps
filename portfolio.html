My Portfolio

Here are a few examples of what we've done with Grand Circus.

GC Cafe This is a project I am very proud of. We worked as a group to create a working Cash Register Terminal to take in a User order, display said order in the form of a bill, take in a payment method and return a receipt.

Deck of Cards This was a recent project for website creation. I developed code that produced a webpage displaying 5 cards from a saved Deck. You may draw a whole new hand or choose to keep specific cards. After the whole Deck is used, you may use the "Reshuffle" to replenish the deck.

Pig Latin "The project that started it all" so to say. This was one of our first labs and quite the hurdle to overcome. Essentially it takes in a word, breaks it down based on set parameters and adds the correct connotation to translate to Pig Latin.
